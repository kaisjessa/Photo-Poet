Welcome to Your Boy

From pictures to words. Within seconds.

Learn More

Upload a Pic

All you need to do is upload a picture of your choosing; it can be of food, a plant, or yourself! Images can be uploaded with a variety of extensions by simply pressing the button below.

Wait a Few Seconds

In just a few seconds, it will be processed by two different machine learning algorithms, one that will extract features from the picture and another that will create a poem out of them!

Read Away

Your custom-generated poem will be displayed under the picture you uploaded! This way, you'll be able to see the correlation between the two and enjoy some AI-generated literature!
