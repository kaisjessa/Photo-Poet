Welcome to PhotoPoet!

From pictures to words. Within seconds.

Learn More

Upload.

All you need to do is upload a picture of your choosing; it can be of food, a plant, or even yourself! Images can be uploaded with a variety of extensions by simply pressing the button below.

Wait...

In just a few seconds, it will be processed by the Clarifai API that will extract a feature from the picture. This feature will then be sent to our machine learning model that will write a poem about it!

Read!

Your custom-generated poem will be displayed under the picture you uploaded. This way, you'll be able to see the correlation between the two and enjoy some AI-generated literature!
